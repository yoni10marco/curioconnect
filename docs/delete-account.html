CurioConnect — Account Deletion

You can request the deletion of your CurioConnect account and all associated data at any time.

How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Send an email to our support address
    Use the email address associated with your CurioConnect account.
  2. Use the subject line: Delete My Account
    This helps us process your request promptly.
  3. Include your registered email address in the message body
    So we can locate and verify your account.
  4. We will confirm deletion within 7 business days
    You will receive a confirmation email once the deletion is complete.
📧 Send your request to: curioconnecforyou@gmail.com

What Data Will Be Deleted

Data Type Action
Account credentials (email, password)Deleted immediately
Profile information (username, age, job title)Deleted immediately
Learning interestsDeleted immediately
Daily lessons and quiz historyDeleted immediately
XP points and streak dataDeleted immediately
Lesson queue (pre-generated lessons)Deleted immediately
Push notification tokensDeleted immediately
Feedback submitted to CurioConnectRetained for up to 90 days (anonymized)
Note: Anonymized, aggregated analytics data (not linked to your identity) may be retained for up to 90 days for service improvement purposes. No personally identifiable information is kept after account deletion.
